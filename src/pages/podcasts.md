## The UNSONG Audiobook

[unsong.libsyn.com](https://unsong.libsyn.com/)

I narrated a fantasy/alternate history novel, "UNSONG", with the permission of the author, Scott Alexander of the Slate Star Codex/Astral Codex Ten blog.

Two-hundred and forty-four thousand, one hundred and forty words. Thirty-two hours of audio. I did all the voices, some of which involved sound effects. Completed from 2016 through 2020.

This production might be the one thing I am most widely known for outside of the Detroit area. All-time downloads as of 2025: 215,419; 3,482 of which were just this month.

The podcast includes short stories by the same author. It is complete, and so went on indefinite hiatus.

Whether you listen to it or not, I hope you'll read [UNSONG: Afterword From The Narrator](https://matt-arnold.com/blog/2022-09-26-unsong-afterword-from-the-narrator/), one of my favorite things I've ever written.

## Fluidity Audiobooks

[fluidity.libsyn.com/](https://fluidity.libsyn.com/)

Many of the books are partially-complete. The podcast will return from hiatus when more of the content is written.
